Chhattisgarh

Rice Bowl of India

Chitrakote Falls

Often called the "Niagara Falls of India," Chitrakote Falls is the broadest waterfall in the country, with a majestic horseshoe shape.

Kanger Valley National Park

A biodiversity hotspot known for its dense forests, unique geological formations, and diverse wildlife, the park is located in the Bastar district.

Bastar

Located in southern Chhattisgarh, the Bastar district serves as the gateway to the state's tribal heartland and is rich in cultural heritage.
